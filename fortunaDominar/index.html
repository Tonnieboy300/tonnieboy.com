Fortuna Dominar

A card game

  • Enter the world of Fortuna Dominar and take the reigns of an oppresive dictatorship.

  • Erect buildings and gain resources.

  • Attack enemy states and steal their territory.

  • Try to maintain your Oppression Tokens as the people fight back.

Game Specs

Required Parts

  • One d12

  • Oppression Tokens

  • Bread Tokens
